What areas in Ho Chi Minh City do you deliver to?

expand_more

We deliver to all districts within Ho Chi Minh City. Delivery fees may vary based on the distance from our store. Please check our delivery policy page for detailed information on zones and charges.

How can I keep my flowers fresh for longer?

expand_more

To maximize the lifespan of your flowers, trim the stems at an angle before placing them in a clean vase with fresh water. Keep them away from direct sunlight and heat. Change the water every two days for best results.
